== snaprestore.fail.volume.size
*Severity*::
ALERT
*Description*::
This message occurs when a SnapRestore(R) operation on a volume fails due to insufficient space in the target volume to hold the data present in the snapshot.
*Corrective Action*::
Resize the target volume to the recommended minimum size.
*Syslog Message*::
SnapRestore operation failed: volume %s is too small to hold the data present in the snapshot. Minimum size of the target volume required is %s.
*Parameters*::
*volume* (STRING): Volume name.
*volsize* (STRING): Recommended minimum size of the target volume.
